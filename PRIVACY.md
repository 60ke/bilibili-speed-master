# Privacy Policy for Bilibili Speed Master

**Effective Date:** May 18, 2026

Bilibili Speed Master ("we," "our," or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**Bilibili Speed Master does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII).**

To provide its core functionality, the Extension stores only the following technical preferences locally on your device:
- **Playback Speed Preference:** Your last selected playback speed (e.g., 3.0x).
- **Persistence Toggle:** Whether you want the extension to remember your speed across videos.

This data is stored using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API and stays strictly on your local machine. We do not have servers, and we never upload this data anywhere.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The Extension operates only on `bilibili.com` domains. It does not communicate with any third-party servers, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

## 3. Data Security
Since no data is transmitted or stored on any external servers, your information is as secure as your local browser environment.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating this document on our official GitHub repository.
